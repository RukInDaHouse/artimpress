Courses

Principles

The work of the Art Impress school continues the traditions of Russian and foreign academic schools. Claiming and preserving the priority of fundamental academic principles in drawing, painting, graphics, sculpture and photography, which are common to any realistic image, Art Impress teachers in their professional activities teach to overcome a number of specific challenges, which contemporary artists face in their creation process: complex and simple at the same time, beautiful as according to their idea, and in the manner of execution.

Tradition

Each class is thought out and aims to achieve exact goals for development and growth of our students. Our teachers pay special attention to the drawing, which is commonly taken as a measure of professionalism of artists who created works in various materials and techniques and lived at different times.

Drawing

Drawing is the keystone, the foundation on which fine arts have been built from ancient times to present days. We take drawing as a mandatory subject for detailed and deep study while preparing individual educational plans for our students. We take drawing as a mandatory subject for detailed and deep study.

Individual

Intense bidirectional student-teacher communication is a necessary component of the educational process and a mutually enriching factor in the development of artists as a harmonious creative personalities. Individual educational plans are composed on the basis of the goals and the objectives, which are set by the student, also taking into account the student's previous experience.
